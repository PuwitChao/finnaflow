# FinnaFlow User Guide

## Professional Financial Visualization & Privacy-First Asset Management

Welcome to **FinnaFlow**, your professional-grade financial command center. This guide covers how to maximize the tool's advanced features while maintaining 100% data privacy.

---

## 1. Intelligent Ingestion (Batch Paste)

Stop manually typing expenses. Intelligent Ingestion uses locally-run heuristics to extract transactions from raw text.

- **How to use**:
  1. Open your bank app (e.g., K PLUS, SCB, GrabPay).
  2. Copy the transaction text from your history or statement.
  3. Click **"Batch Paste"** in the FinnaFlow header.
  4. Paste the text. The system automatically values (e.g., "GrabPay *Ref 1234" becomes "GrabPay").
  5. **Indentation Check**: For statements that indent income and expenses differently (like K PLUS), the system is optimized to detect the transaction type based on whitespace and keywords (e.g., "รับโอนเงิน" as Income).
  6. Review, toggle types if needed, and click **Import Selected**.

## 2. Dynamic Batch Editing

Managing hundreds of entries is effortless with the built-in multi-select engine.

- **How to use**:
  1. Click on individual items in your Income/Expense or Assets/Liabilities lists to select them.
  2. Use the **"Select All"** toggle at the top of any list for rapid bulk changes.
  3. A **Floating Batch Toolbar** will appear at the bottom.
  4. Use the **"Change Category"** dropdown to move all selected items (e.g., move 20 "Other" items to "Wants") in one click.
  5. Click **"Delete Selected"** to bulk-remove items.

## 3. Financial Freedom (FIRE) Tracker

Plan your early retirement using the 4% Safe Withdrawal Rule.

- **Target Pot**: Calculates your "Freedom Number" (Monthly Expenses × 300).
- **Current Velocity**: Automatically uses your current Net Worth and Monthly Surplus from the visualizer.
- **Time to Freedom**: Projects exactly how many years and months remain based on your expected ROI (e.g., 7% market average).

## 4. Projection Mode (Resilience Engine)

Simulate economic shifts without affecting your core data.

- **Inflation Stress-Test**: See how your "Unallocated Surplus" shrinks if costs rise by 5-10%.
- **Market Shocks**: Simulate a 30% drop in dividend income to test your financial safety floor.

## 5. Privacy & Data Security

FinnaFlow is a **Local-First** application.

- **Zero Servers**: Your financial data never leaves your device.
- **Redaction**: Names and IDs are automatically removed during Batch Paste to keep your imports clean.
- **Offline Exports**: Save your state as a `.json` file for backup, or export a `.csv` for use in Excel/Sheets.
